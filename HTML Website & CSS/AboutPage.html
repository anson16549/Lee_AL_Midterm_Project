- COME SEE WHAT'S HAPPENING -

DrinkSer Belgian White Belgian-Style Wheat Ale was born in Denver’s baseball stadium,
Coors Field, in 1995. For more than 20 years, the Sandlot brewery provided our team of
brewers an opportunity to create and share new beers with fans around the world from Denver.

In the summer of 2016, DrinkSer brought the same creativity and passion for beer to our
new state-of-the-art brewery and restaurant in Denver’s RiNo District. Since then we have
been brewing fan favorites (old and new), and experimenting with new and unique ingredients
on our pilot brewing system. With 20+ rotating taps, we invite you to pull up a barstoo
and enjoy one of our special brews.

DrinkSer is proudly brewed at the Sandlot, at the RiNo Brewery, and at breweries throughout the world.

PRIVATE EVENTS

Whether setting up a meeting for 10 people in our private event space or a reception for 150 on the north side of the restaurant, we will work with you to execute a great event.
